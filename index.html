Seenapse

Seenapse is an inspiration engine generated with artificial intelligence. This web application lets people share their mental associations about any topic. It is a brainstorming tool that combines the experience and knowledge of people with artificial intelligence to create new ideas (Seenapses).
This web app was made with React.js. I worked on the Front-End of a new feature during October and November of 2021. The beta version of this feature was released in December 2021. This allows registered users to get AI generated ideas in minutes. I generated the necessary components for the new feature, including a form, and a component that renders the results of a query to the AI API.
